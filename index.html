About Karen Tsang & Co., CPA

Founded by Karen Tsang, CPA, CA, our firm was built on a simple belief: every client deserves personalized attention, honest advice, and measurable results.

For over 20 years, we've partnered with individuals, entrepreneurs, and businesses across a wide range of industries — providing the financial insight and strategic guidance they need to thrive. We're not just accountants; we're trusted advisors who genuinely care about your financial well-being.
